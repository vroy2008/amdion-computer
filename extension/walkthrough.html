AMDION

Chrome is wired up.

Amdion now works with your real Chrome — your profile, logins, and extensions stay exactly as they are. A couple of optional touches make it calmer.

Checking connection to Amdion…
1

Quiet your new-tab page

Chrome doesn't let an extension change this for you, so it's a 10-second manual step: open a new tab, click the ✎ Customize (pencil) button, and turn off cards / feed. Fewer suggestions, less pull.

2

Block ads & trackers with uBlock Origin Lite

A lighter, quieter web. Install uBlock Origin Lite from the Chrome Web Store (Amdion can't install it for you). The classic uBlock Origin is no longer offered for Chrome.

Get uBlock Origin Lite
3

Pick your mode

Click the Amdion button in Chrome's toolbar to switch between Off (just track), Nudge (a gentle card), and Block (block distractions outright). When the Amdion app is connected, your mode follows the intent you set there.

Everything Amdion sees about your browsing stays on your device.
