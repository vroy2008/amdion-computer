Reading

Reader theme
Default for Read Mode. Adjust per-article from inside the reader.
Show “Read” pill
A quiet prompt on article pages. The ⌃⇧R hotkey works either way.
Lock distractions while reading
Blocks your distraction sites in Chrome until you leave the reader, then restores your friction setting.
Offer Present on long pages
On a long page the reader can’t reformat, a one-tap offer to go fullscreen with distractions locked. Off by default.

Reshape

Calm distraction sites
Quiet feeds, rails, and badges — and a gentle nudge when you drift. Works even with friction Off.
Fade the feed past the fold
Dim the bottomless scroll on X & LinkedIn after the first screenful; click to bring one back. Experiment.
Hide the YouTube home grid
Keep search and Subscriptions; hide only the algorithmic home wall.
